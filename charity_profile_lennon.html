Lennon Pregnancy Center

What We Do

Taken from the Lennon Website: The Lennon Pregnancy Center offers non-judgmental counseling, material assistance, and education before, during and after pregnancy. The Center feels an obligation to women who, through our influence, chose to continue their pregnancy and provides its supportive services for up to five years following the birth of the baby. The Center desires to break the cycle that often is passed down from generation to generation and to assist women in becoming the best mothers they can be.

Why You Should Volunteer

If you are looking to volunteer and help mothers and families in need then this is the place for you! Here you can make an impact and help these women and families in their pregnancy and beyond.
