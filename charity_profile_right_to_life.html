Right to Life Michigan

What We Do

Taken from the Right to Life Michigan Website: Right to Life of Michigan (RLM), our 80+ affiliates, and thousands of volunteers from all walks of life seek to build a society which welcomes and supports life at all stages. RLM offers tools and resources, staff experts, and volunteers committed to helping you build a practical path forward, bringing hope and happiness in making a choice for life. We are here to stand with you. No one in Michigan should feel they are alone when faced with a decision for life: a personal decision, a consideration to help a friend, a hope to educate your school or community or just a question to learn more. We aim to walk alongside you!
