Center for the Works of Mercy

What We Do

Taken from the Center for the Works of Mercy Website: The Center for the Works of Mercy in Detroit exists to change lives by providing food, clothing, and healthcare services to individuals with low incomes. This one-stop haven of help and hope offers services and programs in one location.

Why You Should Volunteer

This charity is aimed towards families in desperate need of help and resources. If you are passionate about helping these families who are so desperatly in need, consider volunteering here!
